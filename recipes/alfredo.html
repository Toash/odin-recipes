Fettucini Alfredo

Description

Very delicious

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 1.75 cups heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • 1.5 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter
  2. Add heavy cream, salt and pepper, and minced garlic. Keep on low heat
  3. Add parmesan cheese
  4. eat
